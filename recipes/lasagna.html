Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it's make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

  • creamy ricotta mixture
  • savory meat sauce
  • lots of mozzarella cheese.

Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles).
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover and bake at 375Â° for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired.
  7. Let it rest for 15 minutes prior to serving with Garlic Bread With Cheese.
